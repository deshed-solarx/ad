basic infomation about the game:


the game is about a tower, being the player, defending itself against hordes of zombies. featuring regular zombies on the first wave with a default gun to defend the tower, it starts off easy. upon killing the sufficient amount of zombies to pass the wave, you progress to an upgrade, where you can either pick the first or second upgrade. both upgrades are randomly picked from the pool of available upgrades and will change your gun in significant ways. every new wave requires more kills, gives more score, and has stronger zombies. there isnt only one type of zombie, theres the fast zombie, ranged zombie, tank zombie, necromancer zombie, and the skeleton. when you lose, your score is converted into coins which is used to buy permanent upgrades (like permanently shooting 2 bullets instead of 1) to give you the upper hand.
